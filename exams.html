Top Exam Preparation Tricks

Learn the smartest ways to prepare for exams and score higher with less stress.

✔ Trick 1: Make a Timetable

Plan your study hours with break time. Stick to your schedule.

✔ Trick 2: Focus on Important Chapters

Check previous year questions to know exam patterns.

✔ Trick 3: Use Active Recall

Close the book and try to remember what you learned.

✔ Trick 4: Solve Mock Tests

Mock tests boost speed, confidence, and accuracy.

]

✔ Trick 5: Revise Every 48 Hours

Quick revision helps store information in long-term memory.

✔ Trick 6: Stay Calm

Meditation, exercise, and good sleep minimize exam stress.
